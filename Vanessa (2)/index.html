Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

How does delivery work across Sierra Leone? +

We deliver to all 16 districts in Sierra Leone. Delivery within Freetown takes 1-2 business days, while deliveries to other districts take 3-5 business days. You can track your order status in your account dashboard.

What payment methods are accepted? +

We accept mobile money (Orange Money, Afrimoney), bank transfers, and cash on delivery. All payment methods are secure and verified.

Can I return or exchange products? +

Yes! We offer a 7-day return policy for most products. If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, contact us within 48 hours for a full refund or exchange.

Are the products genuine and authentic? +

Absolutely! We work directly with verified suppliers and brands to ensure all products sold on Sierra Walmart are 100% genuine and come with manufacturer warranties where applicable.

How do I create an account? +

Creating an account is easy! Click the user icon in the navigation bar, then click "Create Account." Fill in your details and you'll be ready to shop in minutes.
