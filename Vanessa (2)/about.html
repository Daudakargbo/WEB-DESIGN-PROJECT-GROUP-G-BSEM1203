Our Story

Sierra Walmart was founded in 2024 with a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality products accessible to every Sierra Leonean, regardless of where they live. We recognized that millions of people across our beautiful country faced challenges in accessing diverse products at fair prices.

What started as a small online platform in Freetown has grown into Sierra Leone's most trusted e-commerce marketplace, serving customers across all 16 districts. Today, we offer over 10,000 products from trusted suppliers, with reliable delivery to your doorstep.

Our commitment goes beyond just selling products. We're building a digital economy that empowers local businesses, creates jobs, and improves the quality of life for all Sierra Leoneans.
